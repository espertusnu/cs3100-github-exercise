# Chicken Soup

## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken (about 3–4 lbs) or 2 lbs bone-in chicken pieces
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 cups chicken broth or water
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
- Egg noodles or rice (optional)

## Instructions
1. Place chicken in a large pot and cover with broth or water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 45 minutes.
2. Remove chicken, let cool slightly, then shred the meat and discard skin and bones.
3. Skim any foam from the broth. Add carrots, celery, onion, garlic, bay leaf, and thyme.
4. Simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.
5. Return shredded chicken to the pot. Add noodles or rice if using and cook until done.
6. Remove bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley if desired.

# Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 4 oz pancetta or bacon, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta
    water, then drain.
2. In a large skillet, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
4. Add hot spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta, tossing to combine.
5. Slowly pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved
    pasta water as needed to thin the sauce.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.

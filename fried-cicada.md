# 🦗 Southern-Style Fried Cicadas

*Crispy, protein-packed snack — nutty, buttery, and surprisingly delicious*

---

## Ingredients
*Serves 4*

- 2 cups fresh cicadas (blanched, wings removed)
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
- 2 lemon wedges (to serve)

---

## Instructions

1. **Prep the cicadas** — Blanch cicadas in boiling water for 2 minutes, drain, and pat completely dry. Remove wings and any loose legs.

2. **Make the dredge** — Whisk together flour, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Beat eggs in a separate bowl.

3. **Coat** — Dip cicadas in the egg wash, then toss in the seasoned flour until fully coated. Shake off any excess.

4. **Fry** — Heat oil in a heavy skillet to 350°F (175°C). Fry cicadas in small batches for 2–3 minutes until golden and crisp. Do not overcrowd the pan.

5. **Drain & serve** — Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate. Season with a pinch of extra salt while hot, then find the nearest trash to put it in.

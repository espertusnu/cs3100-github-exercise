# Fried Cicada

## Ingredients
- 1 lb cicadas, unmolted
- 1 tsp salt, grinded
- 1/4 cup salt, raw
- 1 cup peanut oil
- 1 tsp ground sichuan pepper

## Instructions
1. Wash the cicada
2. Boil the cicada first with all of the raw salt
3. Wash the cicada
4. Preheat the oil
5. Put the cicada into the oil
6. Fried it golden
7. Filter the oil and get the cicada out
8. Put the salt and ground sichuan pepper
9. Mix them by shaking

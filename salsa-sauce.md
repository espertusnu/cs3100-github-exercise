# Salsa

## Ingredients
- 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 jalapeño (seeded for milder salsa), finely chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- Juice of 1 lime
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions
1. In a medium bowl, combine the tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, and cilantro.
2. Add the lime juice and a pinch of salt, then stir to combine.
3. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt or lime juice as needed.
4. Let the salsa rest for 10–15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld before serving.

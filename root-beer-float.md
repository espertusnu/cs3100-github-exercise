# Root Beer Float

## Ingredients
- Vanilla Ice Cream (4 oz.)
    -plant based milk (Coconut/Almond/Cashew/Soy/Oat) or Lactaid milk based alternatives for the lactose intolerant
- Root Beer (8 oz.)

## Instructions
- Spoon a scoop of vanilla ice cream into tall glass
- Slowly pour root beer into glass, allowing foam to rise
- Serve with straws and spoons

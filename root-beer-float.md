# Root Beer Float

## Ingredients
- Vanilla Ice Cream (4 oz.)
  - Or use 4 oz. plant-based milk (coconut/almond/cashew/soy/oat) or Lactaid milk instead of ice cream for a lactose-free version
- Root Beer (8 oz.)

## Instructions
- Spoon a scoop of ice cream into tall glass
- Slowly pour root beer into glass, allowing foam to rise
- Serve with straws and spoons

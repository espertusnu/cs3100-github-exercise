### Ingredients

* 12 lasagna noodles
* 1 lb ground beef
* 2 cups marinara sauce
* 1 cup ricotta cheese
* 2 cups shredded mozzarella
* 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
* 1 egg
* 2 cloves garlic (minced)
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp black pepper

### Directions

1. Cook noodles according to package; drain.
2. Brown beef with garlic in a pan; drain fat.
3. Stir marinara sauce into the beef and simmer 5 minutes.
4. Mix ricotta, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
5. In a baking dish: layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mix, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
6. Top with Parmesan.
7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–35 minutes.
8. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice and serve.

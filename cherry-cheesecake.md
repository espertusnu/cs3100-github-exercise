# Cherry Cheesecake

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs (about 12 full crackers)
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

### Filling
- 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

### Topping
- 1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until the texture resembles wet sand.
3. Press the crust mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then let cool.
4. Beat cream cheese and sugar together on medium speed until smooth and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing on low after each addition until just combined.
6. Mix in sour cream, vanilla extract, and flour until smooth. Do not overmix.
7. Pour filling over the cooled crust and spread evenly.
8. Bake at 325°F for 55–65 minutes, until the edges are set but the center has a slight jiggle.
9. Turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour (this helps prevent cracking).
10. Remove from oven and run a thin knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the crust.
11. Let cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
12. Spoon cherry pie filling evenly over the top before serving.
13. Release the springform pan, slice, and serve chilled.

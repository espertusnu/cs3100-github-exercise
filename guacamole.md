# Guacamole

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 roma tomato, diced
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional)

## Instructions
1. Halve and pit the avocados, scoop flesh into a bowl.
2. Mash avocados with a fork to desired consistency.
3. Stir in lime juice and salt.
4. Mix in onion, cilantro, garlic, tomato, and jalapeño.
5. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
6. Serve immediately or press plastic wrap directly onto surface to store.

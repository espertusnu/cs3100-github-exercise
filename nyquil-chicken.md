# NqQuil Chicken

## Ingredients
 - 1 whole chicken
 - 1 cup NyQuil
 - 2 tbsp olive oil
 - 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a bowl, mix NyQuil, olive oil, and salt.
3. Place chicken in a roasting pan and pour NyQuil mixture over it.
4. Roast for 1 hour and 30 minutes, basting every 30 minutes.
5. Ensure chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165°F or 74°C).
6. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

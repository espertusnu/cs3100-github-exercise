# Banana Bread

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
2. Mash the bananas in a mixing bowl.
3. Stir in melted butter.
4. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
5. Sprinkle in baking soda and salt, then stir.
6. Add flour and mix until just combined.
7. Pour into prepared loaf pan.
8. Bake 55–65 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
9. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
